About RH Designer Graphics

Built on Precision
& Passion

We're a vehicle wrap studio driven by craft. From the first design mockup to the final squeegee pass, every detail matters — and you'll see that in the finished product.

Our team brings together years of installation experience and a sharp eye for design. We use only premium cast vinyl from top-tier brands, so your wrap looks great and lasts for years.

Whether you're a business owner growing your brand on the road or a car enthusiast after a jaw-dropping color change, RH Designer Graphics delivers work you'll be proud to drive.

How It Works

The Process

01
Consultation

We sit down (or connect remotely) to understand your vehicle, vision, and goals. No pressure — just ideas.

02
Design & Mockup

Our design team renders a full preview of your wrap so you can see exactly what you're getting before we print a single inch.

03
Print & Cut

We print on premium large-format equipment and precision-cut every panel — color-matched and ready for installation.

04
Installation

Our certified installers apply every panel in a controlled environment. You pick up a vehicle that stops traffic.
